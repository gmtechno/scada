TECHNO SCADA FOR PUMPING STATIONS

SCADA systems provide a powerful tool for managing booster pump sets, helping operators to monitor, control, and optimize pump performance while ensuring reliability, efficiency, and safety. With the ability to automate processes, detect faults early, and provide remote management capabilities, SCADA enhances the overall operation of pump systems and can reduce both operational costs and maintenance efforts.

Power Section Monitoring

  • Supply voltage monitoring
  • Current consumption analysis for:
    • Pump 1
    • Pump 2
    • Pump 3 and additional pumps
  • Real-time motor temperature analysis
  • Pump trip monitoring

Control System Features

  • Remote modification of control philosophy
  • VFD Management:
    • Remote speed control
    • Pressure optimization
    • Flow regulation
