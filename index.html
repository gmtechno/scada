TECHNO SCADA Monitoring and Control for Industrial Systems

SCADA (Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition) is a system that enables remote monitoring, control, and automation of industrial processes and infrastructure. It is widely used in applications such as power generation, water treatment, HVAC systems, oil & gas, manufacturing, and transportation. SCADA systems integrate hardware (sensors, actuators, PLCs, RTUs) and software to provide operators with a centralized platform for managing the entire system, improving operational efficiency, safety, and reliability.

Key Functions of Our SCADA Monitoring and Control:

  • Real-Time Data Acquisition: SCADA systems collect real-time data from remote sensors, field devices, and controllers.
  • Visualization and HMI: The SCADA HMI provides a graphical interface for operators to visualize the entire system in real-time.
  • Control and Automation: SCADA systems provide operators the ability to control industrial processes and devices remotely.
  • Data Logging and Historical Trends: SCADA systems store historical data from all sensors and controllers in the database for later analysis.
  • Alarming and Notifications: SCADA systems are designed to generate alarms when system parameters exceed predefined limits.
  • Remote Monitoring and Control: SCADA systems allow centralized management of distributed assets from anywhere via secure access.
  • Reporting and Analysis: Generate routine and custom reports for performance, energy usage, system status, and alarm logs.
